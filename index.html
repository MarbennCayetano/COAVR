City of Austin

Technology that Takes You There...

Explore amazing natural resources such as the many caves hidden within Austin. Experience the life saving side of how an ambulance and it's crew provide services to our residents. Participate as a virtual audience in a City meeting!

Innovation in Education...

VR can provide a unique "hands on" and immersive learning experience that may not be fully replicated through more common, standard techniques.

Expanding Accessibility...

Accessibility to information and services is a City responsibility to our residents where VR can be used to help extend that reach in many scenarios.

Highlights!

  • 2016 Idea Accelerator grant recipient for "Virtual Reality as an Internal Support Service" project.
  • 2017 US Ignite/GigaTECHs App competition winner for "Just In Time VR" - A Texas State University, Emergency Medical Services (EMS), and Communication and Technology Management (CTM) collaboration project for first responder VR training.
  • 2019 Best of Texas Awards - Most Innovative use of Citizen Engagment category, "Virtual Reality Cave Tour" interactive VR website.
